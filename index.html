Intro

Dental care is generally perceived to be expensive by the general public. However it is not so, dental treatment can be done on a relatively tight budget. There are 3 main avenues of receiving dental treatment in order of price, mainly A) Government Clinics; B) Dental Schools; C) Private Dental Clinics.

Dental schools are the best of both worlds. With educational institutions having skilled professors to guide students during the treatment process, yet still having a price point that is affordable to the general public. Although total treatment time by a dental student would be relatively longer, this is neccesary as every procedure is inspected and checked by the professors to ensure that the correct procedures are followed and patient safety is ensured.
